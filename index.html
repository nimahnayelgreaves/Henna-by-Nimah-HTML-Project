About Us

Henna by Nimah was founded based on a love of doing all her wonderful family and friends' henna for years. After developing her craft, she decided to really take herself seriously and pursue her passions.

Our Henna

Nimah lives with exczema, and has her whole life. So have many of her lovely clients. We make sure to use hypoallergenic henna, that is safe for all skin types. HOWEVER, we recommend anyone with concerns to do a patch test first.
